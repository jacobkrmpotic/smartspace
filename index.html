Stage 2

Description:

From our original ideas in stage 1. We ultimately decided to produce a smart home manager called smartspace. Our target demographic would be homeowners. It would be implemented as a physical home console with the possibility of a mobile companion in the future.

Stakeholders

Our stakeholders are primarily; our potential users, as well as the users and owners of existing programs for the smart home.

Research

Research was carried out by surveying local home owners on what they may look for in our program.
